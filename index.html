About Our Hospital

Quality Healthcare,
Close to Home

Begusarai Multispeciality Hospital Pvt. Ltd. is a leading private healthcare institution located in Mahmadpur, Ward No. 38, Begusarai, Bihar — delivering advanced, compassionate, and affordable medical care to the people of Begusarai and surrounding districts.

Our hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art ICU, NICU, a fully modular Operation Theatre, a 24/7 Emergency Room, and an in-house Pathology laboratory — ensuring comprehensive care entirely under one roof.
